Professional Summary

I'm a QA Automation Engineer focused on building test suites that provide clear, reliable signals about system quality.

My work centres on API, UI, and performance automation, with an emphasis on maintainability, reproducibility, and CI visibility. I aim to ensure that test results are not only correct, but also easy for teams and stakeholders to interpret and act on.

My work focuses on ensuring automation provides actionable insights, reducing false positives and improving confidence in release decisions.

I'm particularly interested in designing frameworks that reduce flakiness, improve debugging, and make automation outcomes transparent through publicly verifiable evidence.

I enjoy breaking complex systems down into testable components and making quality visible through data.
